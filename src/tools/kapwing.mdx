Kapwing is a feature-rich online video editor emphasizing team collaboration and user-friendliness. It integrates a multitude of AI tools to make the video creation process more automated and efficient, eliminating the need for cumbersome desktop software downloads.

## Key Features

*   **Smart Cut:** Automatically removes long periods of silence from videos, saving time on manual trimming.
*   **Automatic Subtitle Generation:** Quickly generates subtitles for videos, supporting various languages and styles.
*   **AI-Generated B-Roll:** Intelligently recommends or generates relevant B-roll (supplementary footage) from its media library based on your video content, enriching the visual narrative.
*   **Online Collaboration:** Supports multiple team members editing and commenting on a single project simultaneously, ideal for collaborative workflows.

Suno is a globally impactful AI music generation tool. Users can simply input a descriptive text prompt (e.g., "a melancholic pop song about walking in the rain"), and Suno will generate a complete song, including vocals, lyrics, and instrumental accompaniment, in a short amount of time.

## Key Features

*   **Text-to-Song Generation:** Its core functionality transforms natural language prompts into high-quality musical compositions.
*   **Custom Mode:** Allows users to provide their own lyrics and specify music styles, enabling the AI to compose and perform the music.
*   **Diverse Music Styles:** Supports dozens of music genres, including pop, rock, folk, electronic, and hip-hop.
*   **Instrumental Mode:** Beyond generating songs with vocals, it can also produce purely instrumental background music or accompaniments.

Pictory.ai is an AI-powered video creation platform designed to help content creators quickly produce short, engaging videos from various text sources. It automates the video editing process, making it ideal for repurposing existing content for social media, marketing, and educational purposes.

## Key Features

*   **Text-to-Video Conversion:** Automatically converts long-form content (blog posts, articles, scripts) into concise video summaries.
*   **Automatic Visuals:** Selects relevant video clips, images, and background music from a vast library based on your text.
*   **AI-Generated Voiceovers:** Provides realistic AI voices to narrate your video content.
*   **Automatic Captions:** Generates and synchronizes captions for enhanced accessibility and viewer engagement.
*   **Brand Kit Customization:** Allows users to add their own branding, intros, and outros to maintain brand consistency.

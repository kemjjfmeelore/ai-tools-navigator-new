Cleanvoice AI is a dedicated "cleanup" tool for audio post-production. It focuses solely on perfecting recorded audio files using AI, without offering recording or complex editing functionalities. It is particularly suitable for podcasters and video creators who demand high-quality audio.

## Key Features

*   **Filler Word Removal:** Intelligently detects and removes common filler words and verbal tics (like "um," "uh," "you know") in multiple languages.
*   **Mouth Noise Removal:** Automatically identifies and eliminates distracting mouth noises such as clicks, pops, and smacks that affect listener experience.
*   **Silence Trimming:** Automatically shortens or removes overly long periods of silence in audio tracks, making conversations sound more concise and engaging.
*   **Multi-language Support:** Its algorithms support the detection of filler words in various languages, including English, German, French, and Chinese.

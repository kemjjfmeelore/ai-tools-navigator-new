AIVA (Artificial Intelligence Virtual Artist) is an advanced AI composer capable of generating original and emotionally rich musical soundtracks. It is designed for various media applications, including film scores, video game music, advertising jingles, and any project requiring unique and expressive background music.

## Key Features

*   **Original Compositions:** Creates unique musical pieces from scratch based on user specifications.
*   **Emotional Customization:** Users can guide the AI to compose music reflecting specific emotions or atmospheres (e.g., epic, tranquil, melancholic).
*   **Genre & Style Variety:** Offers a range of musical styles and genres, from orchestral and cinematic to modern electronic.
*   **Customization Options:** Provides tools to adjust mood, instrumentation, and duration of the generated tracks.
*   **Royalty-Free Music:** Generated compositions can be used royalty-free, making it ideal for commercial projects.

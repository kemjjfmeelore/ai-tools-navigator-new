Soundraw is an AI music generator that empowers creators to produce custom, royalty-free music for their projects quickly and easily. It offers a wide array of options to tailor the music to specific moods, genres, and lengths, making it ideal for video content, podcasts, and presentations.

## Key Features

*   **Customizable Music Generation:** Allows users to choose mood, genre, instruments, tempo, and duration to generate unique music.
*   **Royalty-Free Library:** All generated music is royalty-free, suitable for commercial use without licensing concerns.
*   **AI-Powered Editing:** Provides tools to fine-tune generated tracks, including rearranging sections and adjusting elements.
*   **Vast Style Options:** Offers numerous categories like Cinematic, Corporate, Happy, Sad, etc., to match specific project needs.
*   **Seamless Integration:** Music can be easily downloaded and integrated into various creative projects.

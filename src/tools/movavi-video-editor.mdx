Movavi Video Editor is a user-friendly desktop video editing software that has integrated a variety of AI-powered tools to simplify and enhance the editing process. It caters to both beginners and experienced editors looking for smart automation features without moving to complex professional suites.

## Key Features

*   **AI Noise Reduction:** Automatically cleans up audio by removing unwanted background noise.
*   **AI Background Removal:** Quickly removes backgrounds from videos without green screen, ideal for creating dynamic effects.
*   **AI Motion Tracking:** Allows users to track objects or faces in a video and attach elements (like text or graphics) to them.
*   **AI Upscaling:** Can improve the resolution of videos, enhancing visual quality.
*   **One-Click Highlight Reel:** AI can help identify and compile the best moments of your footage into a highlight reel.
*   **Intuitive Interface:** Known for its easy-to-navigate interface, making advanced editing accessible.

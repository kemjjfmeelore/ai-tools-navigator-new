Opus Clip is a powerful assistant for content creators, specifically designed to address the challenge of repurposing long-form video content. It leverages AI to analyze extended videos (such as podcasts, interviews, or lectures) and automatically clips out the most engaging segments with high potential for virality on social media.

## Key Features

*   **AI Smart Analysis:** Capable of understanding video content, identifying key insights, highlights, and compelling topics.
*   **Virality Score:** Assigns a score to each clipped segment, helping creators gauge its potential for becoming a "hit" on social media.
*   **Auto Reframe:** Intelligently recognizes faces and keeps them centered in vertical video formats, eliminating the need for manual adjustments.
*   **Automatic Captions:** Generates dynamic and eye-catching captions for videos, significantly improving viewer engagement and completion rates.

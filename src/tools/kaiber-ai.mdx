Kaiber.ai is an innovative AI video generation platform focused on artistic and stylized visuals. It allows creators to produce unique animations and videos from simple inputs like text descriptions, images, or even audio tracks, transforming ideas into captivating visual experiences.

## Key Features

*   **Text, Image & Audio to Video:** Generate videos based on textual prompts, existing images, or visualize audio.
*   **Diverse Artistic Styles:** Offers a wide range of artistic styles and filters to apply to generated videos, from anime to oil painting to cyberpunk.
*   **Music Visualization:** Can create dynamic visualizers that react to audio inputs, perfect for musicians and artists.
*   **Customizable Animation:** Provides controls over camera movement, animation intensity, and visual effects.
*   **Creative Exploration:** Ideal for artists, musicians, and filmmakers looking to experiment with AI-generated visuals.

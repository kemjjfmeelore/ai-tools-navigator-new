HeyGen is a leading AI video generation platform particularly adept at creating videos with realistic "digital humans" (AI Avatars). It is highly suitable for producing video content such as corporate training, product introductions, and marketing promotions that do not require human presenters.

## Key Features

*   **Extensive Avatar Library:** Offers over 100 AI avatars of diverse ethnicities, ages, and styles, with options to create custom avatars.
*   **Text-to-Video:** Simply input your script, select an avatar, and quickly generate a video of the avatar presenting your content.
*   **Voice Cloning & TTS:** Supports cloning your own voice by uploading a voice sample, or utilizing its built-in library of high-quality Text-to-Speech (TTS) voices.
*   **Video Translation:** A revolutionary feature that can translate the speech and synchronize the lip movements of a person in an existing video into another language, producing very natural results.

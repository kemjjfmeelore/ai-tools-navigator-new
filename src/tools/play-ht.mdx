Play.ht is a comprehensive AI voice generation platform that provides creators with access to an extensive library of realistic Text-to-Speech (TTS) voices and advanced voice cloning features. It's designed to help convert text into natural-sounding audio for a wide range of applications, including audio articles, podcasts, e-learning, and video voiceovers.

## Key Features

*   **Vast Voice Library:** Offers over 900 AI voices in 140+ languages and accents, providing diverse options for any project.
*   **Realistic TTS:** Generates high-quality, human-like speech from text with customizable styles, emotions, and pronunciations.
*   **Voice Cloning:** Allows users to create a unique AI voice model by cloning an existing voice from a short audio sample.
*   **Audio Article Creation:** Automatically converts blog posts and articles into audio versions, making content more accessible.
*   **Podcast & Video Voiceovers:** Ideal for creating professional voiceovers without the need for recording equipment or voice actors.

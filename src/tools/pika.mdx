Pika is a highly regarded AI video generation tool known for its powerful features and creative community. It aims to lower the barrier to video creation, enabling anyone to transform their ideas into lively video content with simple prompts.

## Key Features

*   **Multi-modal Generation:** Supports generating new video content from text, images, or even existing video clips.
*   **Inpainting (Region Modification):** Allows users to modify specific areas of a video, similar to Photoshop, for tasks like "changing a character's outfit" or "adding a cat to a scene."
*   **Video Length Extension:** Can intelligently extend the duration of existing video clips while maintaining coherence.
*   **Canvas and Aspect Ratio Control:** Offers various canvas size options (e.g., 16:9, 9:16) to help creators customize content for different social media platforms.

iZotope RX is widely recognized as the industry-standard software for audio repair, enhancement, and restoration. While not exclusively an AI tool, its latest versions deeply integrate machine learning to tackle complex audio problems, from background noise removal to dialogue isolation, making it indispensable for audio engineers, podcasters, and filmmakers.

## Key Features

*   **AI-Powered Noise Removal:** Advanced algorithms to intelligently remove various types of unwanted noise, including hum, hiss, clicks, and environmental sounds.
*   **Dialogue Isolate:** Separates dialogue from noisy backgrounds, making speech clearer and more prominent.
*   **De-reverb:** Reduces or removes unwanted reverberation from recorded audio.
*   **Spectral Repair:** Visually identifies and repairs individual audio imperfections like mouth clicks, phone rings, or mic bumps.
*   **Music Rebalance:** Allows for rebalancing levels of vocals, bass, drums, and other instruments in a mix.
*   **Comprehensive Suite:** Offers a complete set of modules for every aspect of audio post-production.

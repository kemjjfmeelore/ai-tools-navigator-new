ElevenLabs stands as a benchmark in the Text-to-Speech (TTS) domain, celebrated for the naturalness, emotional richness, and realism of its generated voices. Whether used for video voiceovers, audiobooks, or digital avatars, it delivers industry-leading vocal performance.

## Key Features

*   **Hyper-realistic TTS:** Its core technology generates human-like speech with emotional nuances and intonation.
*   **Voice Cloning:** Requires only a small audio sample to clone a person's voice, which can then be used to read any text.
*   **AI Voiceover & Translation:** Can translate a spoken audio (e.g., English) into another language (e.g., Spanish) while preserving the original speaker's unique voice characteristics.
*   **Sound Effect Generation:** A recently added feature that allows generating various sound effects from text prompts.
